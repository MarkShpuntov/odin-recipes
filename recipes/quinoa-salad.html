Quinoa Salad

Description

A delicious and filling open-faced sandwich with chicken,avocado, and egg.

Description

  • Bread
  • Chicken Breast
  • Egg - Sunny side up
  • Avocado
  • Mayo

Steps

  1. Fry chicken breast
  2. Fry egg (sunny side up)
  3. Put mayo and ketchup on bread
  4. Put chicken, avocado, and egg on bread
  5. Top with Frank's hot sauce
